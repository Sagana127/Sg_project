Facts

Space:

A day on Venus is longer than a year on Venus. It takes about 243 Earth days to rotate once on its axis, but only about 225 Earth days to orbit the Sun.

Animals

Octopuses have three hearts. Two pump blood to the gills, while the third pumps it to the rest of the body. When they swim, the heart that delivers blood to the body stops, which is why they prefer to crawl.

History

Cleopatra lived closer in time to the first Moon landing than to the construction of the Great Pyramid of Giza. The pyramid was built around 2560 BCE, while Cleopatra lived around 69-30 BCE.

Language

The word "set" has the highest number of different meanings in the English language, with over 430 definitions listed in the Oxford English Dictionary.

Food

Honey never spoils. Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old and still perfectly edible.

Nature

Bananas are berries, but strawberries are not. Botanically, a berry is a fruit produced from the ovary of a single flower with seeds embedded in the flesh.

Technology

The first computer virus was created in 1983. Called "Elk Cloner," it was designed to infect Apple II systems and spread via floppy disks.
